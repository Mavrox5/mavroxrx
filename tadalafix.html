Dosage & Use

Take it once. Forget about it.

01

Take once daily, same time each day

Consistency is everything with daily dosing. Take your tablet at the same time each morning or evening, with or without food.

02

No timing required

Unlike on-demand treatments, there's nothing to take before intimacy. Tadalafix maintains a steady level in your system, so you're always ready.

03

Start at 2.5mg, adjust as directed

Most patients begin at 2.5mg per day. Your provider may increase your dose to 5mg based on your response and tolerability. Never adjust your dose without guidance.

04

Allow a few days to establish

Daily Tadalafil builds up over several days. Give it at least a week of consistent use before evaluating whether the dose is right for you.

Important: Do not take Tadalafix with nitrates, alpha-blockers, or if you have been advised against sexual activity. Do not exceed your prescribed dose. Contact your provider if you experience prolonged side effects.
