Guarantee & Refunds

Mavrox Money Back Guarantee

Mavrox offers a money back guarantee. You may be eligible for a refund or program credit if you fully comply with all program requirements and do not achieve at least a 10% reduction in your baseline body weight within nine months. Full compliance with the program requirements — such as regular check-ins, proper use of medication, and adherence to lifestyle and coaching recommendations — is necessary to qualify for this guarantee.

By participating in the program, you agree to these terms and conditions and acknowledge that adherence to the program and accurate reporting are essential for eligibility.

Please note that participation is subject to change at any time. We reserve the right to cancel the program or update these terms at our discretion, with changes applying to all future requests or claims.

Participation in any medication-based weight-loss program requires medical evaluation and ongoing oversight by the Provider Group. All determinations regarding eligibility, medication appropriateness, dosing, titration, continuation of therapy, or discontinuation are made exclusively by the Providers. Mavrox does not control or influence these medical decisions.

The administrative requirements listed in this program — including logging activity, engaging with coaching resources, completing educational modules, and timely participation in the Mavrox platform — are membership obligations and do not constitute medical advice.

Who Qualifies for the Guarantee?

  • Start Date: You must have begun your medication with Mavrox on or after February 12, 2025.
  • First-Time Use: This must be your first experience using the eligible medication class (GLP-1s, Bupropion+Naltrexone, or Metformin+Topiramate+Naltrexone). Prior use of these medications (compounded or branded) disqualifies you.
  • Medical Suitability: You must complete a medical evaluation and obtain approval through Mavrox's network of Providers to ensure you are an appropriate candidate for treatment. This includes providing accurate health and weight information.

Program Commitment

To qualify for the guarantee, you must fully adhere to the following:

  • Medication Protocol: Take your prescribed medication for the duration of the program as directed by your Provider. Medications must be ordered through the Mavrox Member Portal.
  • Provider appointments: Attend all required medical follow-up visits.
  • Educational requirements: Complete all assigned learning modules in the Mavrox Member Portal.
  • Check-ins: Complete monthly check-ins through the Mavrox Member Portal.
  • Weight reporting: Log your weight weekly in the Mavrox Member Portal.
  • Community participation: Post at least once a week in the Mavrox Facebook Community.

Medication-Specific Requirements

All medication dosing protocols must be followed as prescribed by your Provider. The following additional requirements apply by medication type:

1. Injectable GLP-1s (Semaglutide or Tirzepatide)

  • Medication must be taken continuously for 9 months, as prescribed.
  • You must be actively working with a Mavrox Health Coach.
  • No more than two (2) missed virtual or text-based coaching sessions.

2. Oral GLP-1 (Semaglutide or Tirzepatide)

  • Medication must be taken continuously for 9 months, as prescribed.
  • You must be actively working with a Mavrox Health Coach.
  • No more than two (2) missed virtual or text-based coaching sessions.

3. Other Weight-Loss Medications (e.g., Naltrexone + Bupropion, Metformin + Naltrexone + Topiramate)

  • Medication must be taken continuously for 9 months, as prescribed.
  • You must attend two (2) coaching sessions per month with your Mavrox Health Coach (included in your program).
  • No more than two (2) no-shows or late cancellations.
  • You must actively work toward personalized goals assigned by your Coach.
  • You must complete all assigned educational resources on time.

Medication Program Disclaimer

All medication programs are determined by your licensed Provider. Program length, dose, and medication changes are based on medical appropriateness and are at the sole discretion of your Provider.

Continuing on medication for the full 9 months is not guaranteed. Ongoing treatment is determined by your Provider based on medical necessity, effectiveness, and safety. If your Provider discontinues medication for any reason before the 9-month period ends, you may still remain eligible for the Mavrox Weight Loss Guarantee by continuing with a Mavrox Health Coach for the remainder of the 9-month program.

Failure to Achieve Weight Loss

If, after meeting all the above requirements, you fail to lose at least 10% of your baseline weight (as recorded during your initial intake), you may qualify for Mavrox's Weight Loss Guarantee Program.

Exclusions

You are excluded from the Mavrox Weight Loss Guarantee if:

  • You are not a first-time user of the eligible medication class.
  • You have a personal or family history of medullary thyroid cancer (MTC) or MEN 2.
  • You have Type 1 diabetes.
  • You have active eating disorders (e.g., anorexia, bulimia, binge eating).
  • You have severe gastrointestinal issues (e.g., gastroparesis, IBD).
  • You have significant kidney or liver disease.
  • You have a history of pancreatitis or unmanaged gallbladder disease.
  • You are pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • You have had bariatric surgery within the past 12 months.
  • You use medications that conflict with treatment.
  • You have uncontrolled psychiatric conditions that affect compliance.
  • You are non-adherent to treatment, dosing, coaching, or appointment requirements.
  • You experience significant hormonal changes during the program.
  • You use other weight-loss methods or medications not prescribed by Mavrox during the program.
  • Eligibility is determined by the prescribing Provider based on medical history and clinical guidelines, and you fail to meet any of the program requirements listed above.

  • Each weight entry must be time-stamped and submitted consistently on the day it is recorded.
